# Mission: Durable Objects

## Why

Zeke is a Cloudflare DevRel engineer who currently uses Durable Objects through cognitive surrender:
he can spin them up but doesn't know what the runtime does on his behalf. His skip-level manager
challenged him to resolve this — both for his own confidence and to build empathy for users who
feel the same way.

## Success looks like

- Can recommend or reject DOs with clear reasoning ("use a DO when X, not when Y")
- Can write a DO from scratch and explain every line — what it does and why the runtime needs it
- Can explain to a confused developer why DOs feel like magic and what the magic actually is

## Constraints

- 15 minutes per lesson session
- Quizzes + hands-on coding (both)
- Primary sources only: Cloudflare docs, not pre-trained LLM knowledge

## Out of scope

- Agents SDK (abstraction layer on top of DOs — learn the primitive first)
- Project Think / Flu (adjacent agent frameworks)
- DO pricing, limits, and production ops (save for after the fundamentals land)
